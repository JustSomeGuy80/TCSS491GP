Controls

Walk (a and d)

Moves the player to the left or right respectively

Jump (space)

Makes the player jump upwards

Fire (left click)

The player will shoot a bullet in the direction of the mouse cursor (this move shares its cooldown with slash)

slash (s)

The player will slash in the direction of the mouse cursor (this move shares its cooldown with fire)

teleport (w)

The player will teleport in the direction of their velocity (holding w will display where you will be teleported)
